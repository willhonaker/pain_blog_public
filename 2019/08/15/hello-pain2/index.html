Goals for the blog

This is my second blog, and it’s about pain. I’m definitely writing for myself at the moment. There are millions and millions of blogs out there, but this is the only one about my struggles with PAIN. If nothing else, this blog will be some long overdue therapy.

Some longterm goals (aka, being painfree)

Some personal pains I’ve had for the last ten years or longer:

  • Low back pain
  • Knee pain (mostly in right knee)
  • Neck pain
  • Vascular headaches
  • Tension headaches
  • Jaw pain
  • Achilles pain
  • Allergies and sinus pain
  • Pain of existence

Am I too pain focused? Perhaps. Stay painfree.
